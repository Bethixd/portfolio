my work

"Fossilized"

Designing the UI for a conceptual travel app

The brief was to create 5 travel app screens. I decided that for mine i would create an app that helped children educate themselves on natural history and also know where the nearest museums and archaeological digs are around them - allowing them to explore outside and learn new information.

Personal branding

Designing the brand identity for my own personal brand

We were asked to create a range of different things for our brand - including a monogram, wordmark, visual marque, brand guidelines, establish a tone of voice, decide on colours, and create touchpoints.

Branding "Pizza Passion"

Designing the branding for a conceptual pizza restaurant

for this brief we were asked to create a brand in a week for either a preexisting pizza shop, or a concept pizza shop of our own. This task involved establishing a tone of voice, and then using the tone of voice to create visual designs for the brand such as a monogram/ symbol, a wordmark, digital touchpoints such as these app screens, and a physical touchpoint.
